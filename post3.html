LIAM CARROLL THE SALEM RED SOX VERSION OF TED LASSO

By Scott Weaver | April 13th, 2023



Salem, Virginia, meet Great Britain’s version of Ted Lasso, the lovable, mustachioed American football coach who found his way across the pond to coach a struggling English football team. His upbeat attitude, constant pop culture references, and folksy wisdom won over the hearts of fans and players alike. But what happens when we compare him to a new addition to the baseball world, Liam Carroll, the former GB Baseball Head Coach who is now managing the Salem Red Sox? Let’s find out!

First off, it’s worth noting that Ted and Liam come from two very different worlds. Ted was a football coach who found his way to soccer, while Liam has been involved with baseball since 1996. But both have a certain charm and charisma that make them easy to root for.

Ted is known for his relentless positivity and his ability to find the good in every situation. Even when his team is struggling, he maintains his upbeat attitude and always puts his players first. Liam, on the other hand, is more of a no-nonsense kind of guy. He’s tough but fair, and he expects his players to give 100% every time they step onto the field. Coach Liam’s long term goal for the Salem Red Sox is to develop his players. He wants them to increase their baseball IQ every time they step on the field.

But despite their differences, Ted and Liam share a love for their respective sports and a dedication to their teams. They both understand that winning isn’t everything, but that it’s important to strive for excellence in everything they do.

When it comes to coaching style, Ted and Liam have some similarities as well. They both believe in building strong relationships with their players and creating a positive team culture. Ted famously says, “Be a goldfish,” meaning to have a short memory and move on from mistakes quickly. Liam, on the other hand, emphasizes the importance of discipline and hard work.

One area where Ted and Liam differ is in their use of pop culture references. Ted is known for dropping references to everything from Star Wars to The Simpsons, while Liam is more likely to reference classic baseball movies like The Sandlot or Field of Dreams. But both coaches understand the importance of connecting with their players and using humor to break the tension when things get tough.

At the end of the day, both Ted and Liam are excellent coaches who bring a unique perspective to their respective sports. Ted’s unorthodox approach may not work for everyone, but it’s hard to argue with his success. Liam, on the other hand, is a seasoned veteran of the baseball world who brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the table, and it’s equally hard to argue with his victories.

So, whether you’re a fan of Ted Lasso or Liam Carroll, one thing is clear: both coaches are dedicated to their teams and to the sports they love. And who knows, maybe we’ll see Liam dropping some pop culture references in the dugout this season. After all, a little bit of whimsy never hurt anyone. Red Sox fans, meet your UK Ted Lasso…Liam Carroll.
